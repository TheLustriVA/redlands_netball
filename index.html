Key Game Specifications (U12)

Ball Size

Size 5 - This is the full-size netball used for players aged 10 and over.

Goal Post Height

3.05 metres (10 feet) - Standard regulation height for players aged 11 and over.

Match Duration

Likely 4 x 8-minute or 4 x 10-minute quarters. (Refer to RNA Competitions Manual for specifics). Breaks: 2 mins (1st/3rd Q), 4 mins (half-time).

Team Composition

Seven players on court. Squads typically up to twelve players.

Spirit of the Game

Netball is built on fair play, respect, and safety. Redlands Netball Association (RNA) emphasizes these through its Codes of Behaviour.

  • Treat everyone with respect and courtesy.
  • Be ethical, fair, considerate, and honest.
  • Play within the rules and spirit of netball.
  • Cooperate with everyone involved.
  • Applaud good play from all teams.
  • Prioritize child safety and welfare.

Focus for U12s: Skill development, enjoyment, and fair participation are key, aligning with Netball Australia's "Learn to Train" philosophy. Experiencing different positions is encouraged.

Positive Sideline Culture: Cheer, clap, and encourage all players. Avoid coaching from the sidelines.
